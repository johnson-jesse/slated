🎬 Slated [slated-snowy.vercel.app](https://slated-snowy.vercel.app)

A mobile-first digital clapperboard for YouTube creators and video production workflows.

Slated brings a real-world slate experience into the browser—built for fast-paced shooting, take tracking, and structured content creation. It’s designed to feel like on-set gear, not a web app.
